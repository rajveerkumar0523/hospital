FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about using MediCore.

How do I book an appointment? +

Log in to your patient account, go to Find Doctors, select your preferred specialist, and choose an available time slot. You will receive a confirmation on your dashboard immediately.

Can I cancel or reschedule an appointment? +

Yes. Go to your Patient Dashboard and find the appointment under Your Appointments. You can cancel any pending or confirmed appointment before the scheduled time.

How do I access my lab test results? +

Lab results are available in your Patient Dashboard under Lab Reports as soon as the laboratory technician uploads them. You will see the full report with result values and status.

What should I do in a medical emergency? +

Use the Emergency button on the home page or from your dashboard at any time. Our emergency team is available 24/7 and will coordinate immediate response and ambulance dispatch.

Is my medical data secure and private? +

Yes. MediCore is HIPAA compliant and uses encrypted connections for all data. Your records are only accessible to you and your assigned medical team.

How do I view my prescriptions? +

All prescriptions issued by your doctor are available in your Patient Dashboard under Prescriptions. You can view diagnosis, medicines, dosage, and follow-up dates.
